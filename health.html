Health Benefits

Dark chocolate, especially with 70% or more cocoa, offers several health benefits when eaten in moderation.
  • Rich in antioxidants (flavonoids) that protect cells from damage
  • Supports heart health by improving blood flow
  • May help lower bad (LDL) cholesterol
  • Contains minerals like iron, magnesium, and zinc
  • Can improve mood due to theobromine and serotonin-related compounds
  • May enhance brain function by improving circulation

Possible Risks

Although beneficial, excessive consumption can cause health issues.
  • High in calories and may lead to weight gain
  • Contains caffeine and theobromine, which may disturb sleep
  • Some brands have high added sugar
  • May raise blood sugar if consumed in large amounts
  • Can trigger migraines or acid reflux in sensitive individuals
  • Poor-quality cocoa may contain trace heavy metals
Moderation and quality selection are important.

Nutrition

Nutrients in dark chocolate include carbohydrates, fats, protein, and water.
